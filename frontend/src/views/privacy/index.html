Privacy Policy for TabEase Tab Management Extension

Last Updated: March 12, 2024

This privacy policy ("Policy") describes how TabEase ("TabEase," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our tab management extension (the "Extension").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information:

When you sign up for TabEase, we collect the following personal information: your username, email address, and password. This information is necessary for account creation and authentication purposes.

1.2 Tab Information:

TabEase provides the option for users to group their tabs. If you choose to group your tabs, we collect the URL of each tab and its name for the purpose of organizing and managing your tabs efficiently.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Personal Information:

We use your personal information to create and manage your TabEase account, provide you with access to the TabEase service, and communicate with you about your account and any updates or changes to our service.

2.2 Tab Information:

The tab information we collect is used solely for the purpose of organizing and managing your tabs within TabEase. We do not use this information for any other purposes.

3. Disclosure of Your Information

3.1 Legal Compliance:

We may disclose your information if we are required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with legal requirements or protect the rights, safety, or property of TabEase, our users, or the public.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

5. Your Choices

You can choose not to provide certain personal information, but this may limit your ability to use certain features of TabEase.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at support@tabease.co.

By using TabEase, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to be bound by its terms.
